Gift Information

In Alabama, property is divided into two types: Real Property (land, houses, or anything permanently attached to the land) and Personal Property (all other assets, such as vehicles, furniture, or bank accounts). You can distribute your property through a Specific Gift (designating a particular property, like a house at a specific address or a specific item, to one or more beneficiaries) or a Blanket Gift (giving all your real and personal property equally to one or more beneficiaries).
